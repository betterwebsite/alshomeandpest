Leaf Stoppers

Leave the Clogs Behind: Gutter Guards Keep Your Home Dry & Rodent-Free

When gutters are clogged with leaves, twigs, and other debris, water can’t flow properly away from your home. This pooling water can lead to roof leaks, mold growth, and even structural damage if it seeps under your roofing materials. Installing leaf stoppers or gutter guards ensures that rainwater drains smoothly, protecting your home from expensive moisture issues and saving you time and hassle down the road.

Block Rodents and Insects

Leaf debris in your gutters doesn’t just trap water—it also attracts insects and ants looking for a food source or a damp nesting spot. Rodents like mice and rats see these insects as a tasty snack and are drawn in as well. Once they’re in your gutters, it’s just a short hop for them to find a way inside your attic or walls. By adding gutter guards, you remove the welcome mat for critters and pests alike, cutting off one of their favorite access routes and helping keep your home pest-free.

Act Now to Protect Your Home

Every day you put off installing leaf stoppers or gutter guards is another day for debris and pests to build up and create problems. The sooner you make the upgrade, the faster you’ll enjoy peace of mind knowing your home is safeguarded against water damage, insects, and rodents.
